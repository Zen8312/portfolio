The Challenge

The client's administrative team was struggling with manual data entry across multiple Excel sheets. Tracking student records, normalizing subject data, and matching student IDs was a time-consuming process highly prone to human error. They needed a streamlined interface that non-technical staff could use to automate this workflow seamlessly.

The Solution

I architected a clean, one-page web dashboard using semantic HTML, structured CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript. By connecting this frontend to a lightweight Python ETL backend, I created a "drag-and-drop" interface. Staff could upload raw `.csv` files, and the system would automatically clean, map, and output processed reports.

The Impact

Manual data processing time was reduced from 15 hours per week to just 15 minutes. Error rates dropped to near zero.
